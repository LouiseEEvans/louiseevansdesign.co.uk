The Bite of the Knife

Object-based research and essay

—

As part of year one study on the MA Graphic Media Design course at LCC, we conducted object-based research starting with a single item from the archives.

 

These selected images document the research process; from taking cues from the object itself, to wider material, historical and social research, via Bee Wilson's brilliant Consider The Fork to a finished illustrated essay.
